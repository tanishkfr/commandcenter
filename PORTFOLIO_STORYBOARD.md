# Portfolio storyboard

Target: a 6–8 minute case-study read and a 75-second motion walkthrough.

## Ten frames

1. The loss — transcript length without current reasoning.
2. The thesis — reviewed consequences should remain.
3. The discarded dashboard and why it failed.
4. Conversation → capture → judgment → context.
5. A new decision conflicts with an earlier direction.
6. Keep alongside versus change direction.
7. Current memory and earlier direction remain connected.
8. One model across UI, API, storage, search, export, and MCP.
9. Test evidence plus participant findings when available.
10. What failed, what remains hypothetical, and the next decision.

## Motion walkthrough

Resume a project, continue a thought, capture ordered candidates, choose change direction, inspect the earlier direction and provenance, then undo. Use real content, no fake metrics, no feature montage, and no explanation that depends on music.

Required images: conversation with cited memory; capture sequence; related-memory review; current/earlier pair; provenance; grouped search; architecture; and research synthesis after the study. Every image needs a caption stating the design decision it proves.
